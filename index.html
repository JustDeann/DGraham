About Deann

I am a marketing technology rockstar!

I own the CMS, CDP, and CRM ecosystems that keep lead generation and customer engagement running smoothly, and I still love rolling up my sleeves to design, code, and build the digital machinery behind it all.

For over 20 years, I've led multi-brand web platforms, MarTech integrations, and marketing operations across aviation, insurance, and publishing. Basically, if it has pixels and a process, I've probably helped make it work better. Whether modernizing enterprise sites, connecting analytics and automation, or coaching teams, I focus on building digital ecosystems that perform, convert, and make the complicated stuff feel easy.

From Film to Digital Platforms

Before moving fully into web and marketing technology, I spent several years in independent film production and post-production, earning credits on IMDb. I handled everything from shot lists and storyboards to editing, finishing, and collaborating with directors and artists to tell clear, visually engaging stories.

I also created animation and motion graphics pieces, along with logo and promotional design that blended illustration, typography, and movement. That mix of film, animation, and design still shapes how I work today. I think about narrative, timing, and visual hierarchy every time I design a website, map a user journey, or craft a marketing campaign.

Teaching the Web the Right Way

As I moved into web development, I helped create one of the first local programs based on modern web standards. I worked with the Web Standards Project and Opera's initiative to develop curriculum and teach standards-based HTML, CSS, accessibility, and best practices entirely online.

That work earned recognition as Instructor of the Year and later led to my role as Department Chair for two programs: Digital Media Design and Advertising Design. I led a seven-person team, managed budgets, and continuously evolved the curriculum so students were learning the same standards, workflows, and quality expectations used by professional web and digital teams.

Building a Marketing Stack that Matches the Business

At John Wiley & Sons, I evolved into the go-to person who connected web platforms, data, and campaign goals into a single, working marketing system. I helped design and run a shared Kentico-based web platform that supported seven global brands, partnering with marketing, sales, and events teams to align site structure, content, and experimentation with pipeline and revenue targets. I built lead-gen infrastructure, automated journeys, and reporting pipelines that showed teams what worked from first visit through opportunity, and created documentation, standards, and training that turned the platform into a company-wide center of excellence. Along the way I received multiple company awards, including Team Player of the Month, the Wiley Award for Excellence, and the Wiley Silver STAR Award, in recognition of impact across platform strategy, collaboration, and results.

It's Just Deann

If you're exploring new ideas for your CMS, CDP, or CRM, I'd love to connect and see how I can help. I blend visual design, UX, and full-stack .NET development to build and customize CMS platforms that support how your business actually works.
